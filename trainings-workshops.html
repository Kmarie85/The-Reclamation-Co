Trainings & Workshops

SoulStone offers trauma-informed trainings that have already been developed and delivered for caregivers, helping professionals, volunteers, and community partners. Trainings may be offered as they currently exist or adapted to fit the needs, audience, and context of the organization.

Request Training Information Start a Conversation

Stress, Burnout & Nervous System Support

The Soul Reset

Trauma-informed exploration of burnout as a nervous-system response, with practical tools for regulation and sustainability.

Navigating the Overwhelm

Focuses on burnout, stress accumulation, and caregiver fatigue, with strategies to reduce overwhelm and isolation.

Recognizing Triggers & Staying Grounded

Training on identifying triggers and maintaining regulated, ethical responses in high-stress situations.

Boundaries, Identity, and Caregiver Fatigue

Explores over-functioning, identity strain, and trauma-informed approaches to boundaries.

Healing Your Inner Child

Reflective training exploring how early experiences shape adult stress responses and self-talk, with gentle regulation practices.

Caregiver & Child Welfare–Focused Trainings

Staying Grounded in the Storm

Crisis intervention training for therapeutic foster parents focused on de-escalation, safety, and regulation.

Enhanced Fostering 101

Foundational training covering trauma-informed care, licensing expectations, and system roles.

Fostering 201

Practical guidance for foster parents navigating placements, logistics, and required paperwork.

Foster Parent Discipline Guide

Reviews discipline do’s and don’ts, safe alternatives, and licensing risk reduction.

Play With Purpose

Explores how play supports connection for trauma-impacted children and when it may overwhelm.

Lifebooks

Trauma-informed approaches to helping children build identity and narrative coherence.

Holding Space

Training for volunteers focused on emotional safety and presence with children in crisis.

Confidentiality in Foster Care

Covers legal requirements, common risks, and strategies to protect children’s privacy.

Foster Care Licensing & GAL Training

Overview of licensing requirements and collaboration between caregivers and professionals.

TBRI® Caregiver Training Series

Four-part trauma-informed series based on Trust-Based Relational Intervention®.

CORE Teen Training

Trauma-informed program for caregivers parenting teens, addressing grief, regulation, and transitions.

Custom & Adaptable Curriculum

In addition to these existing trainings, SoulStone develops custom curriculum in collaboration with organizations and communities. Content can be adapted based on audience, setting, timeframe, and identified needs, while remaining grounded in trauma-informed, nervous-system-aware principles.

  • Audience-specific adaptations
  • Single-session or multi-session formats
  • Community, agency, or faith-based settings
  • Needs identified by the organization

Format & Availability

  • In-person, virtual, or hybrid
  • Offered by request
  • Scheduled based on capacity and alignment
