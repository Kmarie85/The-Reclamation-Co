Donor FAQ

We want giving to feel clear, respectful, and aligned — not pressured. Here are answers to common questions.

Talk with Us About Giving Financial Transparency

Is SoulStone a nonprofit?

Yes. SoulStone, Inc. is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization committed to trauma-informed, community-centered support.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Donations to eligible 501(c)(3) organizations are generally tax-deductible in the United States. For questions about your situation, consult a tax professional.

Where does my donation go?

Donations support the mission and help sustain trauma-informed programming, resources, community supports, and operational capacity. You can review financial information on our Financial Transparency page.

Can I give in honor of someone?

Yes. If you’d like to give in honor or memory of someone, reach out and we’ll make sure it’s handled thoughtfully.

Can I donate goods or services?

Sometimes. In-kind support depends on current needs and capacity. Use the contact form and select “Donation” to start a conversation.

Can I sponsor a program or training?

Yes — sponsorship and partnership options may be available depending on programming. Reach out and we’ll talk through what’s realistic and aligned.

How can I request a receipt?

If you need a receipt or documentation, contact us and we’ll help you get what you need.
